f(w)=∑k=2wf(⌊wk⌋)f(w)=\sum\limits_{k=2}^wf(\left\lfloor\frac{w}{k}\right\rfloor) f(w)=​k=2​∑​w​​f(⌊​k​​w​​⌋)
